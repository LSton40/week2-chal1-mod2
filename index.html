Purpose

This site is dedicated to designing and developing websites and apps committed to language learning, maintenance, and revitalization, serving communities whose languages are at risk of being lost.

There are hundreds of apps and websites out there that aim to teach Indigenous languages. Most of them only focus on a few hundred vocabulary items and simple phrases using a simple read, listen, and repeat model for memorization. My goal is to try to develop apps that use diverse methods to present more comprehensive, effective, and well-rounded language lessons in a fun and approachable way in order to build better fluency.
